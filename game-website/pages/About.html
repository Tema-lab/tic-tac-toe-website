Rules / How to play

Welcome to the basic tic-tac-toe game! The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. You are X(cross) , your friend is O(circle) . Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. Only the mouse is used for playing. Scores will be shown in the top left/right corner of the window. Each winning round scoreboard will be updated and the playground will be refreshed. First player who reaches 10 scores wins. After reaching 10 scores Winning text will be shown on top of the playground.
